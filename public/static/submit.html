Submit Your Story

Welcome to Tell Me 'Bout It, an app that collects stories of breakups so we can all feel better about own worst breakups.

Have you been broken up with in the worst, weirdest or simply amusing possible way? Did you break up with someone in a way you thought was pretty bad or maybe even pretty amazing? Have you done some really crazy stuff while trying to get over a breakup? Tell us about it! You can call in your story, record it and share it with others on this site.

Here's how you do it:

Step 1: Prepare your story. You will have 45 seconds to record your story.

Step 2: Call this number: (347) 983 1841.

Step 3: An answering machine will walk you through the process of submitting a story. When prompted, you will have to enter your personal code:

This is your personal code: 123456

Your unique personal code is the way to link up your phone call with your contact information and written story portion below. You may call as many times as you want using the same personal code, but once you click submit at the bottom of this page, your last recording will be the final one.

Step 4: When prompted, record your story. You will have 30 seconds to finish your story. Hang up when you are finished. If you would like to record your story again, repeat steps 1-3.
